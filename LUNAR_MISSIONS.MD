# Lunar Missions Manifest

## TL;DR
This document tracks active and upcoming lunar missions, providing timeline context for LORS standardization efforts.

## Mission Index

### Pre-2025 Commercial Missions
*   [Beresheet 1](missions/BERESHEET_1.MD) - Crashed (Apr 2019). First private attempt.
*   [HAKUTO-R M1](missions/HAKUTO_R_M1.MD) - Crashed (Apr 2023). First commercial lander in lunar orbit.
*   [Peregrine M1](missions/PEREGRINE_M1.MD) - Failed (Jan 2024). First CLPS mission.
*   [IM-1 (Odysseus)](missions/IM_1_ODYSSEUS.MD) - Success (Feb 2024). First successful commercial soft landing.

### 2025 Missions
*   [Blue Ghost M1](missions/BLUE_GHOST_M1.MD) - Landed (Mar 2025). First fully successful commercial soft landing.
*   [IM-2 (Athena)](missions/IM_2_ATHENA.MD) - Landed (Mar 2025). First commercial rover at south pole.
*   [HAKUTO-R M2](missions/HAKUTO_R_M2.MD) - Landed/Deployed (Jan 2025). Micro-rover regolith extraction demo.
*   [Starship HLS Test](missions/STARSHIP_HLS_TEST.MD) - Planned. Uncrewed HLS landing demonstration.

### 2026 Missions
*   [IM-3 (Nova-C)](missions/IM_3_NOVA_C.MD) - Planned. Distributed autonomy/swarm coordination.
*   [Griffin M1](missions/GRIFFIN_M1.MD) - Planned (Jul 2026). Delivering [Astrolab FLEX](rovers/ASTROLAB_FLEX.MD).
*   [Artemis III](missions/ARTEMIS_III_HLS.MD) - Planned (Sept 2026). First human return to surface.
*   [Blue Moon Pathfinder](missions/BLUE_MOON_PATHFINDER.MD) - Planned (Q1 2026). MK1 tech demo.
*   [Chang'e 7](missions/CHANGE_7_MISSION.MD) - Planned. South pole survey.
*   [Blue Ghost 2](missions/BLUE_GHOST_2.MD) - Planned. Far side mission.

### 2027-2029 Missions
*   [Blue Moon MK1](missions/BLUE_MOON_MK1_M1.MD) - Planned (Late 2027). Heavy cargo delivery.
*   [APEX 1.0 (ispace M3)](missions/APEX_1_MISSION.MD) - Planned. Commercial far-side operations.
*   [LUPEX](missions/LUPEX_MISSION.MD) - Planned (2028). Polar water ice investigation.
*   [Artemis IV](missions/ARTEMIS_IV.MD) - Planned (Late 2028). Astronaut transport.
*   [Chang'e 8](missions/CHANGE_8_MISSION.MD) - Planned. ILRS Testbed.
