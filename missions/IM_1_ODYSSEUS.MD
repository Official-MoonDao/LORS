# IM-1 (Odysseus)

## Overview
First successful commercial soft landing. The mission delivered six NASA CLPS payloads and six commercial payloads to the lunar south pole region.

## Details
*   **Lander:** [Nova-C](../landers/NOVA_C.MD) (Odysseus)
*   **Mission Profile:**
    *   **Launch Vehicle:** SpaceX Falcon 9 (LC-39A).
    *   **Insertion Orbit (GTO):** 185 km x 60,000 km | Inclination: 27°.
    *   **Transit (TLI):** Performed ~19 hours after launch. Duration: **7.7 days**.
    *   **Lunar Orbit (LLO):** 100 km circular orbit.
*   **Connectivity:** 
    *   **Direct-to-Earth:** 2x Low-rate S-band transponders + 2x High-rate downlink transponders.
    *   **Encryption:** **AES-256** secured payload data.
    *   **Ground Network:** Intuitive Machines Lunar Data Network (LDN) using S-Band (2200-2290 MHz).
*   **Payloads:**
    *   **NASA CLPS:**
        *   **LRA** (Laser Retroreflector Array) - NASA GSFC
        *   **NDL** (Navigation Doppler Lidar) - NASA LaRC (Critical backup for landing navigation)
        *   **SCALPSS** (Stereo Cameras for Lunar Plume-Surface Studies) - NASA LaRC
        *   **ROLSES** (Radio Operations on the Lunar Surface) - NASA GSFC
        *   **LN-1** (Lunar Node 1 Navigation Demonstrator) - NASA MSFC
        *   **RFMG** (Radio Frequency Mass Gauge) - NASA GRC
    *   **Commercial/Other:**
        *   **EagleCam** (CubeSat camera system) - [Embry-Riddle Aeronautical Univ.](../rovers/EAGLECAM.MD)
        *   **ILO-X** (International Lunar Observatory) - ILOA
        *   **Omni-Heat Infinity** (Thermal coating test) - Columbia Sportswear
        *   **Lunaprise** (Digital Archive) - Galactic Legacy Labs
        *   **Independence** (Data Center test) - Lonestar Data Holdings
        *   **Moon Phases** (Art Installation) - Jeff Koons
*   **Status:** Success / Partial Function (Feb 22, 2024). Landed at Malapert A (80.13°S, 1.44°E). The lander tripped on a rock upon touchdown and came to rest on its side, but remained functional for ~7 days (discharge to depletion at lunar night).
