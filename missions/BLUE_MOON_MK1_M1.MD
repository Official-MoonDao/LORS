# Blue Moon MK1

## Overview
Pathfinder mission for Blue Origin's MK1 cargo lander. Partially funded by NASA CLPS (CT-3) to demonstrate precision landing and cryogenic propulsion.

## Details
*   **Lander:** [Blue Moon MK1](../landers/BLUE_MOON_MK1.MD)
*   **Propulsion:** BE-7 engine running on **LH2/LOX** (Cryogenic).
*   **Capacity:** Up to **3,000 kg** (3 Metric Tons) to the lunar surface.
*   **Connectivity:**
    *   **Direct-to-Earth:** Continuous X-Band downlink provided by the Blue Moon Pathfinder system.
    *   **Surface Networking:** Supports architectural nodes for 25 Mbps 3GPP/LTE surface links.
*   **Payloads:**
    *   **SCALPSS** (Stereo Cameras for Lunar Plume-Surface Studies) - NASA (Funded under CT-3)
*   **Status:** Planned (Early 2026). Pathfinder mission for the MK1 cargo platform.
