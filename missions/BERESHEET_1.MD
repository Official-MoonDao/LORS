# Beresheet 1

## Overview
First private attempt. Reached lunar orbit. Carried scientific and cultural payloads.

## Details
*   **Lander:** [Beresheet](../landers/BERESHEET.MD)
*   **Payloads:**
    *   **SILMAG** (Fluxgate Magnetometer) - Weizmann Institute/UCLA
    *   **LRA** (Laser Retroreflector Array) - NASA GSFC
    *   **Time Capsule** (Lunar Library/Tardigrades) - Arch Mission Foundation
    *   **Cameras** (High-res Imaging System) - SpaceIL
*   **Status:** Crashed (Apr 2019). Engine failure during descent.
