# Blue Ghost 3

## Overview
Firefly's third lunar mission, targeting the Gruithuisen Domes on the lunar near side.

## Details
*   **Lander:** [Blue Ghost](../landers/BLUE_GHOST.MD)
*   **Target Site:** Gruithuisen Domes.
*   **Payloads:**
    *   Includes 6 NASA-sponsored payloads (Task Order CP-21).
*   **Status:** Planned. Targeting 2028.
