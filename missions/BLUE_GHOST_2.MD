# Blue Ghost 2

## Overview
Far side mission using Firefly's Elytra Dark orbital vehicle and Blue Ghost lander. Primary focus on lunar communications and radio astronomy.

## Details
*   **Lander:** [Blue Ghost](../landers/BLUE_GHOST.MD)
*   **Transfer Trajectory:** ~45 Day Balistic/Direct transit.
*   **Connectivity (Far Side):** 
    *   **Relay:** Requires the **ESA Lunar Pathfinder** satellite for Earth-to-Lander communication.
    *   **Link:** X-Band relay via Pathfinder (Deployed by Fireflyâ€™s **Elytra Dark** orbital vehicle).
*   **Surface Duration:** ~14 Earth Days (Solar powered).
*   **Payloads:**
    *   **Rashid 2** (Rover) - MBRSC (UAE)
    *   **LuSEE-Night** (Radio Telescope) - NASA/DOE/UC Berkeley
    *   **User Terminal (UT)** (Comm System) - NASA
    *   **Lunar Pathfinder** (Relay Satellite) - ESA (Primary relay for mission success)
*   **Status:** Planned. First commercial mission to the lunar Far Side.
