# Lunar Rovers

Lunar rovers are robotic vehicles designed for exploration and surface operations on the Moon. Various organizations worldwide are developing lunar rovers in different stages of readiness, indicating growing interest in lunar exploration.

## Rovers Seen at Space Tech Expo (Bremen, Germany, 2025)

The following lunar rovers were presented at the Space Tech Expo in Bremen, showcasing diverse approaches to lunar mobility:

### ispace
- Status: In development
- Notes: Japanese company developing lunar exploration technologies and services
