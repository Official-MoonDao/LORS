# Blue Moon MK1

## Overview
Blue Moon MK1 is the uncrewed cargo variant of Blue Origin's lander architecture. It is designed to fit inside the 7-meter fairing of the New Glenn rocket.

## Key Features
*   **Capacity:** Can land massive payloads (3 Metric Tons) anywhere on the Moon.
*   **Propulsion:** BE-7 engines (LOX/Hydrogen), high specific impulse.
*   **Role:** Pre-positioning infrastructure for Artemis crew missions (MK2).
