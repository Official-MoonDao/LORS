# Argonaut

## Overview
Argonaut (formerly EL3 - European Large Logistics Lander) is ESA's heavy-lift lunar lander.

## Key Features
*   **Mission:** Support Artemis and future commercial bases by delivering large cargo (food, water, rover equipment).
*   **Launch:** Planned to launch on Ariane 64.
