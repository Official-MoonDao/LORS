# Blue Ghost

## Overview
Blue Ghost is Firefly Aerospace's lunar lander, named after a rare firefly species. It successfully demonstrated its capabilities with a flawless mission in early 2025.

## Missions
*   **Mission 1 (Blue Ghost 1):** Launched Jan 15, 2025. Successfully landed in Mare Crisium on March 2, 2025. Operated for a full lunar day (14 days), delivering 10 NASA CLPS payloads. Markedly achieved the first fully successful soft landing by a commercial entity without tipping or major anomalies.
*   **Mission 2 (Blue Ghost 2):** Planned for 2026. Will deliver the LuSEE-Night radio telescope to the lunar **Far Side**, along with a communications relay satellite (ESA's Lunar Pathfinder).
