# Blue Ghost

## Overview
Blue Ghost is Firefly Aerospace's lunar lander, named after a rare firefly species.

## Missions
*   **Mission 1 (Blue Ghost 1):** Scheduled for 2025. Will deliver NASA CLPS payloads to Mare Crisium.
*   **Mission 2 (Blue Ghost 2):** Planned for 2026. Will deliver the LuSEE-Night radio telescope to the lunar **Far Side**, along with a communications relay satellite (ESA's Lunar Pathfinder).
