# Beresheet

## Overview
Beresheet was the first Israeli lunar lander and the first privately funded mission to reach the Moon.

## Key Features
*   **Cost:** Extremely low-cost mission (~$100M total project).
*   **Result:** Reached lunar orbit but main engine failed during descent (April 2019), leading to a high-velocity impact.
