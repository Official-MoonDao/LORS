# HAKUTO-R

## Overview
HAKUTO-R is a commercial lunar lander developed by ispace (Japan). It is designed to offer frequent, low-cost transportation to the Moon.

## Missions
*   **Mission 1 (M1):** Launched Dec 2022. Successfully reached lunar orbit but crashed during landing attempt (April 2023).
*   **Mission 2 (M2):** "Resilience". Incorporates lessons learned. Carrying the "Tenacious" micro-rover.
*   **Mission 3 (M3):** Planned for 2026 (APEX 1.0, US-led).

## LORS Relevance
*   **Micro-Rover Integration:** HAKUTO-R M2 includes a deployment bay for the Tenacious rover, offering a specific example of "garage" integration.
