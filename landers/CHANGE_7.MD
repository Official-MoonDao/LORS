# Chang'e 7

## Overview
Chang'e 7 is a complex multi-vehicle mission to the Lunar South Pole by China.

## Key Features
*   **Components:** It is not just a lander; it includes an orbiter, a lander, a rover, and a "hopper" (mini-flying detector) to search for water ice in shadowed craters.
*   **International:** Carrying payloads from Italy, France, and others.
