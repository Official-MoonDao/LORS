# Peregrine

## Overview
Peregrine was Astrobotic's first lunar lander.

## Missions
*   **Mission 1:** Launched Jan 2024 (Vulcan rocket). Suffered a propellant leak shortly after separation. It never reached the Moon but operated for several days in space before re-entering Earth's atmosphere. It carried payloads like Iris, Colmena, and physical bitcoin.
