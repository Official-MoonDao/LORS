# Chang'e 8

## Overview
Chang'e 8 is a mission focused on testing technologies for the International Lunar Research Station (ILRS).

## Key Features
*   **ISRU:** Will attempt 3D printing using lunar regolith.
*   **Ecosystem:** Designed to work in tandem with Chang'e 7 to form the basic model of a research station.
