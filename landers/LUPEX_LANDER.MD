# LUPEX Lander

## Overview
The LUPEX Lander is being developed by ISRO (India) to deliver the Japanese (JAXA) LUPEX Rover to the south pole.

## Key Features
*   **Collaboration:** Demonstrates ISRO's growing capability as a delivery provider for international heavy payloads.
*   **Target:** High-latitude polar landing (very difficult lighting conditions).
