# LTV Eagle

## Overview
The "Eagle" is the LTV proposal from the Lunar Dawn team, led by Lunar Outpost with partners like Lockheed Martin, MDA Space, GM, and Goodyear.

## Key Features
*   **Heritage:** Leveraging Lockheed's human spaceflight experience and GM's battery tech.
*   **Tires:** Goodyear metal airless tires.
*   **Autonomy:** Can operate uncrewed for science missions when astronauts are not present.
