# LTV Moon RACER

## Overview
The "Moon RACER" (Reusable Autonomous Crewed Exploration Rover) is developed by the Moon RACER team, led by Intuitive Machines with AVL, Boeing, Michelin, and Northrop Grumman.

## Key Features
*   **Chassis:** Leveraging Northrop Grumman's Apollo LRV heritage.
*   **Tires:** Michelin lunar wheel technology.
*   **Power:** Advanced battery management by AVL.
