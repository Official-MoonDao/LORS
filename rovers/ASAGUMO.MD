# Asagumo

## Overview
Asagumo is a walking rover developed by UK-based Spacebit.

## Key Features
*   **Legs:** 4 spider-like legs.
*   **Sensors:** Equipped with LiDAR and HD cameras for cave/lava tube exploration.
*   **Structure:** CubeSat frame basis with legs attached.
