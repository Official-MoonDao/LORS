# CubeRover

## Overview
CubeRover is a modular rover platform developed by Astrobotic in partnership with CMU. It aims to be the "CubeSat of Rovers".

## Key Features
*   **Scalability:** Designed in "units" (2kg, 4kg, etc.) similar to CubeSats (1U, 2U, 3U).
*   **Standardization:** Offers standard power and data interfaces for payloads.
*   **Heritage:** Derived from years of CMU rover research.
