# CADRE

## Overview
CADRE is a technology demonstration by JPL consisting of a trio of shoebox-sized rovers.

## Key Features
*   **Swarm Autonomy:** The rovers coordinate amongst themselves to explore and map terrain without constant Earth guidance.
*   **Mesh Network:** Demonstrates local comms mesh.
*   **Redundancy:** Distributed risk; if one fails, the mission continues.
