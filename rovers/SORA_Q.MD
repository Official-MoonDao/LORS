# Sora-Q

## Overview
Sora-Q (LEV-2) is a transformable lunar robot developed by JAXA and toy maker Tomy.

## Key Features
*   **Transformer:** Launches as a sphere, splits into two hemispheres to roll on the surface.
*   **Heritage:** Uses toy design principles for miniaturization and durability.
*   **Success:** Successfully deployed from SLIM lander (Jan 2024) and took the photo of the "nose-down" landing.
