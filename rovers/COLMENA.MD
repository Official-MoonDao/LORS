# Colmena

## Overview
Colmena ("Hive") was a mission by the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). It consisted of five tiny robots, each weighing less than 60 grams.

## Key Features
*   **Scale:** Extremely small. Validating that "tiny" robots can survive launch.
*   **Swarm:** Designed to aggregate and measure plasma environment near the surface.
*   **Fate:** Burned up with Peregrine lander (Jan 2024).
