# Lunar Zebro

## Overview
Lunar Zebro is a student-led project from TU Delft (Netherlands). It is distinct for its locomotion.

## Key Features
*   **Legged Motion:** Uses 6 "C-shaped" legs to walk, offering superior obstacle traversing compared to small wheels.
*   **Low Cost:** Designed to be incredibly cheap and robust (swarm potential).
