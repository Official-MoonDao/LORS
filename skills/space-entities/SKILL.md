# Space Entities Expert Skill

## Domain Knowledge
- **File**: `SPACE_ENTITIES.MD`
- **Metadata Format**: YAML blocks with fields: `entity_id`, `name`, `country`, `type`, `specialization`.

## Instructions
1. **Heritage Mapping**: Track which companies have successful mission heritage vs new entrants.
2. **Ecosystem Analysis**: Group entities by their role (Delivery, Mobility, Software, Instruments).
3. **Collaboration Tracking**: Identify international partnerships (e.g., JAXA/ISRO, NASA/Commercial partners).
